Tokyo Ghoul Characters

Special Class Investigators

Special Class Investigators are elite members of the CCG (Commission of Counter Ghoul), known for their exceptional skills in combating ghouls. Here are some prominent members:

CCG
  1. Koutarou Amon - A dedicated investigator known for his unwavering sense of justice and determination.
  2. Kishou Arima - Often referred to as the "Reaper," he is a highly skilled and enigmatic figure in the CCG.
  3. Juuzou Suzuya - An investigator with an unconventional approach and a distinctive style.
  4. Kureo Mado - Known for his intense and relentless methods in hunting ghouls.
  5. Yukinori Shinohara - A veteran investigator with strong leadership and tactical skills.
  6. Iwao Kuroiwa - Recognized for his disciplined approach to ghoul investigations.

Ghouls

Ghouls are creatures that feed on human flesh and have various supernatural abilities. Here are some notable ghouls:

Ghoul
  1. Ken Kaneki - The main protagonist who becomes a half-ghoul after a tragic incident, grappling with his new identity.
  2. Touka Kirishima - A ghoul trying to maintain a semblance of normal life while hiding her true nature.
  3. Tatara - A high-ranking member of the ghoul organization V, known for his strength and influence.
  4. Noro - A formidable and feared ghoul with a mysterious and intimidating presence.
  5. Eto Yoshimura - The leader of the ghoul organization V, known for her intelligence and strategic mind.
  6. Rize Kamishiro - Known for her powerful kagune and significant impact on the series.
  7. Yakumo Oomori - Also known as Jason, he is a brutal and feared ghoul.
